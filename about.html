About the Program

A comprehensive approach to executive development

The Executive Discovery Workshops

The Executive Discovery Workshops is a comprehensive 3-tiered online training program designed to transform participants from aspiring potential business owners into successful executives. Each stage of our program builds upon the previous one, ensuring that participants develop both the mindset and skills required to run a successful company.

Our approach is structured into three distinct stages, each with a unique focus, curriculum, and outcome:

1. The Business Blueprint

Duration: 8 Weeks (Weekly Sessions)

Instructor: Danny Silver

This foundational training focuses on developing the mindset and skills of a business owner. Participants will learn the basics of entrepreneurship, discovering that they can make businesses, and uncover the kinds of business possibilities they can work to accomplish.

2. The Executive Structure

Duration: 3 Months (Bi-weekly Sessions)

Instructor: Justin Grossman

This training advances the participant's skills, diving into deeper operational and strategic areas. It is designed for those who have a basic business, or the basic framework for one, and want to scale it to a professional, sustainable enterprise.

3. The Executive Mastery

Duration: 6 Months (Monthly Sessions)

Instructor: Robert Chun

This final stage is for participants who have mastered the basics and are ready to lead at the highest levels. The training focuses on advanced executive decision-making, leadership in complex environments, and creating a legacy through strategic impact.

How the Workshops are Linked

Each of the three training phases is designed to progressively build on the previous one, ensuring a natural transition from "running a business might be cool," to full executive development. The workshops are not isolated experiences but a continuous journey:

  • The Business Blueprint provides the foundation, teaching participants the basics of ownership and business management.
  • The Executive Structure builds on that foundation, offering intermediate skills to scale businesses effectively.
  • The Executive Mastery is reserved for those who wish to lead at the highest levels, focusing on advanced executive skills and global business strategy.

This ensures that participants are not overwhelmed but are steadily prepared to take on more complex challenges as they advance through the stages. As each training is distinct yet interconnected, participants can choose to progress from one level to the next based on their career goals, or strengthen the level they are in, helping them grow into well-rounded, capable business leaders.

Our Teaching Approach

Expert Instructors

Each level of our program is led by an instructor with proven expertise in their specific area of business development and leadership.

Collaborative Learning

Our workshops feature interactive team activities where participants can share insights, provide feedback, and learn from each other's experiences.

Comprehensive Materials

Each session comes with detailed resources including PDFs, slide decks, worksheets, templates, and instructional videos.

Applied Learning

Participants apply what they learn through practical homework assignments that directly relate to their business goals.

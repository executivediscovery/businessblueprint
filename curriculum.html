Curriculum

Comprehensive breakdown of the Business Blueprint program

Program Overview

The Business Blueprint curriculum is carefully designed to provide a logical progression from fundamental business concepts to practical implementation. Each week builds upon previous lessons, creating a comprehensive foundation for entrepreneurial success.

1

Understanding the Business World

The Truth About Entrepreneurship

Core Topics

  • The entrepreneurial mindset: myths vs. realities
  • Different business models and structures
  • Identifying personal strengths and translating them into business opportunities
  • Understanding market trends and industry dynamics

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what it truly means to be an entrepreneur
  • Identify your own entrepreneurial strengths and areas for growth
  • Recognize various business models and their applications
  • Begin thinking like a business owner rather than an employee

Practical Application

Complete a personal entrepreneurial assessment to identify your strengths, motivations, and potential business areas. Create an initial journal documenting your entrepreneurial journey, which will be built upon throughout the program.

2

Finding Business Opportunities

How to Spot Gaps in the Market

Core Topics

  • Market research techniques and tools
  • Identifying pain points and unmet needs
  • Evaluating competition and market saturation
  • Testing the viability of business ideas

Learning Objectives

  • Develop effective market research skills
  • Learn to spot opportunities that others miss
  • Evaluate potential business ideas objectively
  • Create preliminary criteria for viable business concepts

Practical Application

Conduct basic market research on 2-3 potential business ideas, analyzing demand, competition, and market trends. Develop a simple scoring system to evaluate business opportunities based on market factors.

3

Developing Your Business Idea

Turning Your Passion into Profit

Core Topics

  • Refining your business concept
  • Creating a unique value proposition
  • Customer persona development
  • Product/service market fit

Learning Objectives

  • Transform general business ideas into specific concepts
  • Articulate what makes your business idea unique
  • Understand your ideal customers and their needs
  • Test assumptions about your business concept

Practical Application

Develop customer personas for your target market and craft a compelling value proposition for your business. Create a simple prototype or mock-up of your product/service for preliminary feedback.

4

Crafting a Business Plan

The Blueprint for Success

Core Topics

  • Business plan fundamentals and structure
  • Setting realistic goals and milestones
  • Financial projections and budgeting
  • Resource planning and allocation

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the components of an effective business plan
  • Create a roadmap for your business's first year
  • Set meaningful business goals and metrics
  • Develop preliminary financial projections

Practical Application

Begin drafting your business plan with clear sections for executive summary, market analysis, operational plan, and financial projections. Create a timeline of key milestones for your first year in business.

5

Marketing Foundations

Building Your Brand and Attracting Customers

Core Topics

  • Brand identity and messaging
  • Digital marketing essentials
  • Cost-effective marketing strategies
  • Building a marketing funnel

Learning Objectives

  • Develop a compelling brand identity
  • Create effective marketing messages for your target audience
  • Understand marketing channels and their appropriate use
  • Build a basic marketing plan on a limited budget

Practical Application

Create a brand style guide including logo concepts, color scheme, and messaging guidelines. Develop a 3-month marketing plan with specific channels, content ideas, and budget allocation.

6

Business Operations

Systems and Processes for Efficiency

Core Topics

  • Creating operational workflows
  • Tools and technology for small businesses
  • Managing suppliers and partnerships
  • Quality control and customer service systems

Learning Objectives

  • Design efficient operational systems for your business
  • Select appropriate tools and technologies
  • Develop quality control procedures
  • Create exceptional customer service protocols

Practical Application

Map out your business's operational workflow from product/service creation to delivery. Research and select key tools and technologies needed to start your business efficiently.

7

Financial Management

Budgeting, Pricing, and Financial Sustainability

Core Topics

  • Basic business accounting principles
  • Strategic pricing methodologies
  • Cash flow management
  • Funding options for small businesses

Learning Objectives

  • Understand fundamental financial statements
  • Develop strategic pricing for profitability
  • Create cash flow projections
  • Explore appropriate funding options for your business

Practical Application

Develop a pricing strategy for your products/services based on costs, competition, and value. Create a 12-month cash flow projection and identify potential funding sources if needed.

8

Launch and Growth Strategy

Taking Your Business to Market and Beyond

Core Topics

  • Creating an effective launch plan
  • Customer acquisition strategies
  • Measuring business performance
  • Planning for growth and scaling

Learning Objectives

  • Create a comprehensive launch strategy
  • Develop techniques for acquiring your first customers
  • Establish key performance indicators for your business
  • Understand pathways to growth and scaling

Practical Application

Finalize your business plan including a detailed launch strategy with timeline and execution steps. Prepare a presentation of your business concept and plan for feedback from instructors and peers.

Templates & Tools

Business Blueprint Schedule

The Business Blueprint program consists of 8 weekly sessions, each focusing on a specific aspect of business development. All sessions are conducted live online via Zoom, allowing you to participate from anywhere. Each session includes presentation materials, interactive activities, and Q&A time.

Sessions are recorded and made available to registered participants, but we strongly encourage attending live to benefit from the interactive elements and real-time feedback.

Complete Schedule

Date Topic Description
May 27, 2025 Understanding the Business World Introduction to entrepreneurship, market realities, and identifying your personal strengths as a future business owner.
June 3, 2025 Finding Business Opportunities How to identify market gaps, assess demand, and find profitable business niches that align with your interests.
June 10, 2025 Developing Your Business Idea Techniques for refining your business concept, creating your unique value proposition, and differentiating from competitors.
June 17, 2025 Crafting a Business Plan Building a comprehensive yet practical business plan that will serve as your roadmap to success.
June 24, 2025 Marketing Foundations Creating a marketing strategy, building your brand identity, and learning cost-effective promotion techniques.
July 1, 2025 Business Operations Setting up efficient business operations, managing resources, and creating workflows that save time and money.
July 8, 2025 Financial Management Understanding business finances, budgeting, pricing strategies, and ensuring your business is financially sustainable.
July 15, 2025 Launch and Growth Strategy Planning your business launch, acquiring your first customers, and setting the foundation for sustainable growth.

What to Expect

After completing the Business Blueprint program, participants will be prepared to:

  1. Launch their own business with confidence and a clear strategy
  2. Apply business fundamentals to create a sustainable venture
  3. Continue their executive development journey with Level 2: The Executive Structure program (coming Fall 2025)

Each participant will graduate with a completed business plan, market analysis, and launch strategy they can implement immediately.
